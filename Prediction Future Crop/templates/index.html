What our project does.....

Agricultural markets are inherently volatile due to a variety of factors such as unpredictable weather conditions, fluctuating demand and supply, and global trade dynamics. These fluctuations can have significant negative impacts on the livelihoods of farmers, traders, and the broader economy. Accurately predicting the prices of key agricultural commodities—such as pulses, vegetables, and cereals—remains a challenging task due to the complexity and non-linearity of the factors influencing price changes. Traditional price forecasting models, such as linear regression and time series models, often fail to capture these complexities, leading to suboptimal predictions.

The core problem addressed in this project is the development of a robust, accurate, and efficient price prediction model that can forecast future prices for agricultural commodities. Current models struggle with managing large, multidimensional datasets and providing real-time predictions that reflect dynamic market conditions. There is a need for an advanced approach that not only improves prediction accuracy but also provides stakeholders with timely insights for better decision-making.
