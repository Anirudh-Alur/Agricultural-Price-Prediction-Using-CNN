Our Prediction Algorithms

Convolutional Neural Networks

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is a deep learning model designed to automatically and adaptively learn patterns from data, especially effective for spatial or structured inputs like images or time-series data. In your case, the CNN analyzes market price trends and learns complex features for accurate price forecasting. With a Mean Absolute Error (MAE) of 0.0114, the model demonstrates excellent performance. This implies that the model deviates from actual prices by only about 1.14%, indicating a prediction accuracy of approximately 98.86%. Such precision makes CNNs highly reliable for commodity price prediction tasks.
